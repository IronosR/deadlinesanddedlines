This web-app is made specifically for the struggling people out there.

We have all gone through our struggle on which dealing with hardships and it can take in different forms from person to person. This app is was specifically made so that it can deal with a variety of situations and thus, ticking all the boxes to your workload at once!


Standard Timer

A timer with an option to manipulate your breaks at any time. Suitable for most environments and easy to modify. Our basic Feature.


Project Timer

A timer that can be split into portions of mini timers. Convenient for dealing with smaller tasks of an entire project. The overall timer can be used as a deadline for when a project


Challenge Timer

A unique timer with no alarm. A timer made to maximize effort in a short burst of time. When the time runs out, it will show how late the user is past the set deadline. Suitable for those with limited time. (Omi S, 2022)


Automated Timers

Timers that automatically start according to what was set in a time blocks. Very useful for students and professionals who want to put their focus on their work immediately. (The timer must be set in advance.)


Calendar & Interface Modulatiom

A range of modes and customization options that increase accessibility in supporting the app. Ex: Calendar for applying timers, themes, a colourblind mode (if possible), ringtones (for mobile devices) etc.
